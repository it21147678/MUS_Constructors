Welcome to
M.U.S. Construction (PVT) LTD

M.U.S. Construction is established in 2002 which is a sole proprietor company established in Sri Lanka that caters to various labor supply, civil construction supply works for all items, construction material supply and construction vehicle and machinery hiring.

Get Started

M.U.S. Construction

M.U.S. Construction represents world class brands and product portfolio for all building services requirements across the Commercial, Residential and Hospitality sectors. The company aims to diversify the business scope into potential market segments in the future.

Mr. Mudaligedara, founder of the company started his construction business on the year 2002 as a construction service provider. After his hardworking effort and determination, the company has expanded company relationships with overseas clients as well.

M.U.S. also covers modern technologies to provide total customer satisfaction and on-time delivery of projects.

Wide range of solutions and services including technology

M.U.S. Construction delivers a broad range of construction solutions, combining modern technology with industry best practices—from material supply and labor to civil works and equipment hiring—ensuring efficient, innovative, and client-focused service.

Highly skilled and professional workforce

M.U.S. Constructions strength is its skilled workforce, delivering excellence across all civil construction areas with a strong focus on quality, safety, and efficiency in every project.

Many years of combined experience in the industry

With over two decades of operational excellence since its establishment in 2002, M.U.S. Construction brings a wealth of industry knowledge and experience. The company leadership and team collectively hold extensive expertise, enabling them to undertake complex projects with confidence and reliability.

Least cost solutions, focused on sustainability and backed up by customized maintenance services

M.U.S. Construction is committed to delivering cost-effective solutions that do not compromise on quality or environmental responsibility. By integrating sustainable practices and offering tailored maintenance services, the company ensures long-term value for clients while supporting greener construction outcomes.
