Our Story

M.U.S. Construction is established in 2002 which is a sole proprietor company established in Sri Lanka that caters to various labor supply, civil construction supply works for all items, construction material supply and construction vehicleand machinery hiring.

M.U.S. Construction represents world class brands and product portfolio for all building services requirements across the Commercial, Residential and Hospitality sectors. The company aims to diversify the business scope into potential market segments in the future.

Mr. Mudaligedara, founder of the company started his construction business on the year 2002 as a construction service provider. After his hardworking effort and determination, the company has expanded company relationships with overseas clients as well.

M.U.S. also covers modern technologies to provide total customer satisfaction and on-time delivery of projects.
